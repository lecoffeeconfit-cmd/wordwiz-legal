WordWiz Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WordWiz collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the WordWiz mobile application.

1. Introduction

WordWiz is a vocabulary-learning and study application. This Privacy Policy applies to information processed through the WordWiz mobile application and related services.

By using WordWiz, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create or use a WordWiz account, we may collect information such as:

  • Email address
  • Username or display name
  • Internal account identifier
  • Authentication and account-status information

Passwords are handled through our authentication provider. WordWiz does not store your plain-text password.

App content and learning data

We may store information you create or save while using WordWiz, including:

  • Saved words and vocabulary entries
  • Definitions, notes, examples, and study materials
  • Flashcards or study sets
  • Learning progress and completion information
  • Streaks, scores, statistics, or other progress indicators
  • App settings and preferences

Diagnostic and technical information

WordWiz may collect limited technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app, such as:

  • Crash reports and error messages
  • App version and operating-system version
  • Device type and general technical characteristics
  • Time and location within the app where an error occurred
  • Performance and reliability information
  • Technical identifiers associated with an error event
WordWiz does not use your information for third-party behavioral advertising and does not sell your personal information.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and maintain your account
  • Save and synchronize your vocabulary and study data
  • Provide flashcards, learning tools, and progress features
  • Remember your preferences
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access
  • Diagnose crashes and technical problems
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and usability
  • Respond to support, privacy, or account requests
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Service Providers

WordWiz uses third-party service providers that process information on our behalf to provide app functionality.

Supabase

WordWiz uses Supabase for services that may include authentication, database storage, file storage, and related backend infrastructure. Information stored through WordWiz may therefore be processed by Supabase.

Supabase processes information according to its own privacy and security practices.

Sentry

WordWiz uses Sentry for crash reporting, error monitoring, and app diagnostics. Sentry may receive technical information associated with crashes and errors, such as app version, operating-system information, device characteristics, stack traces, error context, and identifiers associated with an error event.

WordWiz configures Sentry for application reliability and diagnostics. We do not use Sentry for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Apple

When WordWiz is downloaded or used through Apple services, Apple may process information under Apple's own privacy policy and platform terms.

These providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live.

5. When Information May Be Shared

We may share information only in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers that help operate, secure, host, or maintain WordWiz
  • When required by law, legal process, regulation, or valid government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, integrity, and security of WordWiz, its users, or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or transfer of the app or its assets
  • With your direction or consent

We do not sell personal information or share personal information for third-party behavioral advertising.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and learning information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide WordWiz, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect the service.

Diagnostic information may be retained for a limited period needed to identify and resolve technical problems.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or de-identify associated information within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for security, fraud prevention, backups, legal compliance, or other legitimate obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no internet transmission, mobile application, database, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You are responsible for protecting your account credentials and for notifying us if you believe your account has been compromised.

8. Your Privacy Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information associated with you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a portable copy of certain information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

You may also stop future app data collection by deleting WordWiz from your device. Deleting the app does not automatically delete an existing account or information stored in WordWiz's backend.

To request account or data deletion, use the account-deletion option available within WordWiz, where provided, or contact us using the email address below.

We may need to verify your identity before completing certain privacy requests.

9. Children's Privacy

WordWiz is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate authorization.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through WordWiz, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

10. International Users

WordWiz and its service providers may process and store information in the United States and other countries. Those countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your location.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when WordWiz's features, service providers, or legal obligations change.

When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice within the app or through another appropriate method.
